Add manager

Welcome to the Manager Department Dashboard! Adding a new manager to your team is now easier than ever.

You have two convenient options to choose from:

1. Add Manually:

Adding a new manager manually allows you to enter their details directly into our system. Follow these simple steps: Click on the "Add Manager Manually" button. Fill in the required fields. Once you've entered the information, click the "Add" button. The new manager will be instantly added to your team and sended an approval letter to their email.

2. Upload a File:

Got a list of new managers ready in a file? No problem! Our system supports uploads. Here's how: Click on the "Upload File" button. Choose the file containing the manager details. Supported formats include Json and Excel. Read the description which field Click the "Confirm" button, and our system will add all the new managers to your team.
